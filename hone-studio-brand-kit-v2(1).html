01 — Brand Positioning
The Foundation
Hone Studio replaces the need to hire a full ops and tech team. One partner. Strategy, systems, and websites — with zero overhead.
Name

Hone Studio

"Hone" means to sharpen, refine, and perfect — which is exactly what the business does for clients. "Studio" bridges the gap between corporate and creative audiences.

Positioning

Fractional Ops & Tech Partner

Premium, solo-operated consultancy. Not an agency, not a freelancer — a fractional team that embeds into your business and stays until the work is done.

The Swiss Army Knife

One partner, every tool a business needs. The Swiss Army Knife concept is the unifying narrative — used in messaging and copy, not in visual brand identity or logo. It captures the breadth of capability without diluting the premium positioning.

Messaging concept · Not visual identity

11 — Guidelines
Brand Rules
Consistency builds credibility. These rules ensure Hone stays sharp across every scale and application.

Logo Rules

  • Clear space = cap height of "H" on all sides
  • Teal dot is brand identity — never remove it
  • Print min: 20mm wide · Digital min: 100px
  • Icon mark "H" min: 28px

Tone of Voice

  • Direct, not aggressive
  • Expert, not pretentious
  • Warm, not casual
  • Clear, not oversimplified

Illustration Style

  • Linework only — no full illustrations or photos
  • Thin strokes (1–1.5px) in brand accent colors
  • Functional diagrams over decorative art
  • Works for creative and institutional audiences

Strategic Principles

  • Lead with web + automation, not strategy
  • Frame services as outcomes, not deliverables
  • Cross-sell through case studies, not navigation
  • No pricing on site — use "book a call" CTAs

Do's & Don'ts

✓ Do

Use the teal dot as signature brand element
Pair Fraunces display with DM Sans body
Use Space Mono for all labels and tags
Use linework illustrations, not photography
Maintain generous whitespace and calm pacing
Use "Hone your ___" as a copy pattern

✗ Don't

Remove or recolor the teal period
Use more than two accents simultaneously
Set body text in Fraunces or Space Mono
Use stock photos or full illustrations
Use the Swiss Army Knife visually in the logo
List "admin" or "tech support" as standalone services
